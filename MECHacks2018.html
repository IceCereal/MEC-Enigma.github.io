 Tracks:

Rules and Regulations:
  1. This event is free.
  2. Team Size: 2 persons - 4 persons
  3. Not every team will get selected. Teams will get selected only on the basis of their responses in the registration form.
  4. Teams that have been selected will receive an email confirming their participation. Selected teams will be required to show the confirmation email at the gate to participate.
  5. Food (Dinner and Breakfast only) will be provided for free for every participant. Lunch will not be provided. A Food Fest will start from 10:00 AM Saturday where lunch/snacks can be purchased.
  6. Only one member per team needs to register.
  7. Shortlisted teams will receive an email latest by Oct/31.


Rules for Shortlisted Teams:
  1. The reporting time is 16:00. Teams that come early will have to wait at the entrance.
  2. It is mandatory for every member of the team to bring their College ID Card for verification at the entrance.


General Advice and Disclaimers:
  1. It is advised to wear comfortable clothes. The rooms are Air Conditioned and hence, bringing a sweater is advised as well.
  2. Each team is requested to bring their extension board and stationery. Extension Boards may or may not be available during the event.
  3. Teams are to take care of their belongings and mark their belongings with a marker. Enigma - The CS Club or Mahindra École Centrale will not be responsible for the loss of any item.
  4. Damaging any property of Mahindra École Centrale will be met with action.
  5. Parking Space for vehicles will not be provided.
  6. Participants are to wear the wristbands given by the organizers throught the entire event.
  7. At any point throughout the event, the organizers have the final discretion.
